⚔ The History of Stromgarde

Early Stromgarde

Stromgarde was settled in the Ice Mountains of Kalros on the 25th of May, 2017, by koi0001, Volpex, and TheSmilingFlea. The original vision was a neutral dwarven-themed trade town — loyal to no alliance, no person, no cause. That neutrality didn't last long.

As Iblis rose to dominate Kalros, Stromgarde faced a choice: remain neutral and risk annihilation, or seek protection. The town aligned with early Horsia, trading resources and combat power for security — the first step toward becoming a war machine.

Transferal of Leadership

Peace breeds stagnation. With no war on the horizon and no enemies at the gate, the original founders lost interest. No recruitment. No conquest. No reason to log on. One by one, they drifted to other towns and other lives, leaving Stromgarde to quietly die in the frozen mountains.

The ArcherSquid Era

Into the silence came ArcherSquid — new to the server, searching for a town worth fighting for. He found Stromgarde half-dead and saw exactly what it could become. Under his leadership, Teyuh and Inapplicable were recruited, and the town was reborn — no longer a trade post, but a conquest machine.

With a coalition of hardened PvPers and newly recruited towns, Stromgarde overran Iblis. For a time, the north belonged to them.

The Fall

Victory is its own kind of poison. After Iblis was broken, alliance members scattered to other continents chasing new wars and new horizons. The coalition thinned. Iblis regrouped, recruited, and reclaimed Kalros. Stromgarde fell again — not to a single crushing defeat, but to the slow erosion of time and absence.

The Return

Inapplicable never left. For years, through inactivity and political pressure, he held the walls. Stromgarde did not fall while he stood watch.

Now ArcherSquid returns — unbanned, and with unfinished business. What happens next will be remembered on this server for years. The sleeping giant of the north is waking up.

Iblis held Kalros for years. That ends now.
